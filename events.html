Events

At RAGAZZI, we craft more than just exquisite dishes; we curate unforgettable moments that linger on your palate and in your heart. Every event we host is a celebration of flavors, camaraderie, and the joy of being in good company.

Weekly Gatherings

Join us for our beloved weekly gatherings that add a touch of delight to your week.

  • Martini Monday: Unwind and savour the beginning of the week with a selection of delightful $4 Martinis and well drinks at just $3.
  • Oyster Tuesday: Dive into Tuesdays with our irresistible oysters at just $2 each. Enhance your indulgence with Martinis available at $7 and Prosecco at $6.
  • Wednesday Wine Night: Elevate your midweek evenings by enjoying a complimentary 5oz Pinot Grigio or Cabernet with the purchase of any entrée.

Wine Tastings

Embark on a journey through the lush vineyards of Italy with our exclusive wine-tasting events.

  • November 22 — A Night in Tuscany: Immerse yourself in the flavours of Tuscany with a wine-tasting event that celebrates the renowned wines and culinary delights of this picturesque region. Learn more.
  • December 22 — A Tour of Italy: Join us for a wine tasting that travels across the country, sampling the most delightful wines and culinary treasures. Learn more.
  • January 25 — A Night in Veneto: Experience the magic of Veneto with the finest Venetian wines and delectable cuisine this region is known for. Learn more.

Special Entertainment

Enjoy our offerings of exceptional entertainment to accompany your dining experience.

  • November 4 — Partee with Hiiighkee: Join us for a DJ night with Hiiighkee. Enjoy $6 martinis, $3 well drinks, and $6 cocktails from 8:30pm to 1am.
  • October 28, November 18 & 25 — Karaoke Night featuring Cole Foran: Unleash your inner star at our lively Karaoke Nights. Enjoy $4 mixed drinks and $7 cocktails as you sing the night away.

Private Bookings and Catering

At RAGAZZI, every event is a unique tapestry of moments. We invite you to create lasting memories through exclusive celebrations, corporate gatherings, and more.

  • Personalized Dining: Immerse guests with tailored menus crafted to resonate with your needs and preferences.
  • Versatile Spaces: Choose the inviting front room (30 guests), the cozy back room (30), the entire restaurant (60-70), or have us cater to you.
  • Seamless Experience: Enjoy complimentary parking on request or have us set up at a location of your choosing.
  • Connect with Us: To host or cater your extraordinary event with RAGAZZI, call us at 902-444-1199.
