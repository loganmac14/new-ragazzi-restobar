Events

At RAGAZZI, we believe in crafting more than just exquisite dishes; we curate unforgettable moments that linger on your palate and in your heart with the spirit of togetherness. Every event we host is a celebration of flavors, camaraderie, and the joy of being in good company.

Weekly Gatherings

Join us for our beloved weekly gatherings that add a touch of delight to your week.

  • Martini Monday: Elevate your Monday with our curated martini specials, a perfect way to kick off the week.
  • Oyster Tuesday: Dive into Tuesdays with our enticing Oyster Tuesday, where each oyster is a delectable experience at just two dollars each.

Wine Tastings

Embark on a journey through the lush vineyards of Italy with our exclusive wine tasting events.

  • September 25 — A Night in Amalfi Coast: Indulge in the flavors of the Amalfi Coast as we guide you through exquisite wines that capture the essence of this breathtaking region.
  • October 16 — A Night in Piedmont: Join us for a wine tasting that transports you to the Piedmont region, renowned for its exceptional wines and culinary treasures.

Special Entertainment

Enjoy our offerings of exceptional entertainment to accompany your dining experience.

  • September 9 & 30 — Karaoke Night featuring Krazee Kraig: Unleash your inner star as Krazee Kraig hosts our lively Karaoke Nights.
  • Mondays & Tuesdays — Live Music by Rob Reed: Let the melodies of Rob Reed enhance the beginning of your week, as his live performances create the perfect ambiance for your dining pleasure.

Private Bookings

Make your special occasions truly exceptional with our private event offerings. RAGAZZI invites you to create lasting memories through intimate celebrations, corporate gatherings, and more.

  • Customized Culinary Journey: Immerse your guests in a culinary experience tailored to your event as our chefs collaborate with you to curate a menu that resonates, accommodating all dietary needs and preferences.
  • Versatile Spaces: Choose from our inviting front room (seating up to 30 guests), the cozy back room (perfect for 30 guests), or consider booking the entire restaurant (seating 60-70 guests) for a grand affair.
  • Seamless Arrival: For your convenience, enjoy complimentary parking upon request, adding ease to your guests' arrival.
  • Connect with Leonardo: To explore the possibilities of hosting your exceptional event at RAGAZZI, contact Leonardo at 902-440-7725.

At RAGAZZI, We understand that every event is a unique tapestry of moments. Our private bookings fuse Italian flair with personalized attention, ensuring your event is elevated to a new level of sophistication and warmth.
